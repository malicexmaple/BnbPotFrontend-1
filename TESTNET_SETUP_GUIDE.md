# BNB Chain Testnet Setup Guide

## Overview
This guide will help you connect your wallet to BNB Chain Testnet and get free testBNB tokens for testing BNBPOT.COM.

## Step 1: Add BNB Chain Testnet to MetaMask

### Automatic Setup (Recommended)
1. Visit [ChainList.org/chain/97](https://chainlist.org/chain/97)
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Click "Add to MetaMask"
4. Approve the network addition in MetaMask

### Manual Setup
1. Open MetaMask
2. Click the network dropdown (top left)
3. Click "Add Network"
4. Click "Add a network manually"
5. Enter these details:

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Network Name** | BNB Chain Testnet |
| **RPC URL** | `https://data-seed-prebsc-1-s1.bnbchain.org:8545` |
| **Chain ID** | `97` |
| **Currency Symbol** | `tBNB` |
| **Block Explorer** | `https://testnet.bscscan.com` |

6. Click "Save"

## Step 2: Get Free Testnet BNB

### Official BNB Chain Faucet
1. Visit [bnbchain.org/en/testnet-faucet](https://www.bnbchain.org/en/testnet-faucet)
2. Connect your MetaMask wallet
3. Complete CAPTCHA verification
4. Click "Give me BNB"
5. Receive 0.3 tBNB
6. **Limit**: 1 request per 24 hours

### Alternative Faucets (if official is empty)

#### QuickNode Faucet
- **URL**: [faucet.quicknode.com/binance-smart-chain/bnb-testnet](https://faucet.quicknode.com/binance-smart-chain/bnb-testnet)
- **Amount**: Variable tBNB
- **Requirement**: 0.001 ETH on Ethereum mainnet
- **Cooldown**: 12 hours

#### Chainlink Faucet
- **URL**: [faucets.chain.link/bnb-chain-testnet](https://faucets.chain.link/bnb-chain-testnet)
- **Amount**: Multiple tokens including tBNB
- **Cooldown**: Varies

## Step 3: Verify Your Setup

1. Open MetaMask
2. Switch to "BNB Chain Testnet" network
3. Check your tBNB balance (should show ~0.3 tBNB)
4. Visit [testnet.bscscan.com](https://testnet.bscscan.com)
5. Enter your wallet address to verify balance

## Step 4: Test on BNBPOT.COM

1. Visit BNBPOT.COM
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Select MetaMask
4. Approve the connection
5. Create your account (username + email)
6. Agree to terms and conditions
7. Place your first testnet bet!

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Testnet Tokens Have NO Value**
- tBNB cannot be sold or exchanged for real money
- It's only for testing purposes
- Don't send real BNB to testnet addresses

✅ **What You Can Do**
- Test all betting features
- Try different bet amounts
- Win and lose test tokens safely
- Verify the platform works correctly

🚫 **What You CANNOT Do**
- Withdraw testBNB to exchanges
- Trade testBNB for real cryptocurrency
- Use testnet addresses for mainnet transactions

## Troubleshooting

### "Insufficient funds for gas"
- Get more tBNB from faucet
- Wait 24 hours between faucet requests
- Try alternative faucets

### "Wrong network" error
- Make sure you're on BNB Chain Testnet (Chain ID: 97)
- Don't use BNB Smart Chain Mainnet
- Check MetaMask network dropdown

### Transaction stuck/pending
- Check [testnet.bscscan.com](https://testnet.bscscan.com) for status
- Testnet may be slower than mainnet
- Wait 1-2 minutes for confirmation

### Faucet not working
- Some faucets require small amounts of tokens on other chains
- Try multiple faucets
- Check Discord/Telegram for community help
