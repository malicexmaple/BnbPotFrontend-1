How the Jackpot Game Works

Each game runs in rounds. A new round begins when a player places the first bet. As soon as the first bet is submitted, a countdown timer starts (90 seconds by default). During this countdown, other players may join the round by placing their own bets.

If no other players bet:
The round ends when the timer reaches zero, and the single player automatically wins.

If multiple players bet:
All bets contribute to the jackpot pool. Each player’s chance of winning is proportional to the percentage of the total amount they contributed.

Carousel Display System

At the top of the screen, there is a rotating carousel that shows the participants in the round. Each player in the round is represented by a card in the carousel. Each card displays:

Profile picture or avatar

Username

Amount wagered

As more players enter the round, additional cards fill the carousel automatically.

At the moment the round ends, the carousel animates to select the winning card, highlighting the winner in a clear, visually exciting way.

Player List and Bet Information

Below the carousel is a detailed list of all participants in the round. Each row shows:

Player avatar and name

Total wagered amount

The corresponding value in USD

The player’s win probability (percentage based on their share of the total pool)

The total jackpot value is also displayed at the top of the screen and updates live as bets come in.

Winner Selection

When the countdown ends:

The system calculates the winning wallet using a provably-fair algorithm and the weight of each bet.

The carousel spins and lands on the winning player’s card.

The jackpot amount is instantly paid to the winner.

Round Reset
After the winner is chosen:
The new round begins immediately.
The timer is inactive until a new first bet is placed.
Once the first bet lands, the 90-second countdown starts again.
